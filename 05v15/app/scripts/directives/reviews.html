Reviews

Rotten Tomatoes is a review website that rates films on a scale of 0-100%. Films scoring 60-100% are considered Fresh, and 0-59% are considered Rotten.
Roger Ebert rates films on a scale of up to 4 stars, with 4 stars being the best score a film could get.

Rotten Tomatoes was more favorable to the films of 2015, whereas, Roger Ebert gave more stars to the films of 2005.
