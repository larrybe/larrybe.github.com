Board of Directors

Rosina Foust

Rosina Foust is the founder and chairman of the board of Sowing the Seed Ministry.

Prior to funding the organization she was a nurse and has many years of experience working with the people, especially attending to the sick.

Her skills translate into deep empathy for the disadvantaged and is brought to bear on the services provided by Sowing The Seeds Ministry.
