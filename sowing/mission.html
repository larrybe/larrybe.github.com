Mission Statement

Sowing the Seed Ministry is to help feed the homeless, children and single parents. Also the organization will distribute free religious literature to the community. The organization will also help educate orphans. The organization will focus on caring for the whole person to enhance the lifestyle, dignity and quality of life of clients. Above all the organization will assist clients to develop the knowledge, attitude and skills necessary to be self sufficient to maintain their own homes.
