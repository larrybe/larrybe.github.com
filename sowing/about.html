About Sowing The Seed Ministry

Sowing the Seed Ministry is a not-for-profit organization based in Elkhart, Indiana. Our primary goal is to reduce homelessness and hunger in Elkhart and its surrounding towns and cities.

We strive to represent the population of all homeless people and single parents in Elkhart regardless of race, age, gender or disability.
